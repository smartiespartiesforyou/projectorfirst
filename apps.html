Big, Simple Apps & Games

Big, simple, projector-friendly games for everyone.

📌 A Quick Note for Activity Directors

Heads up: Anything you customize inside a game (like your prize list in Let's Make a Deal) saves only on the device and browser where you typed it. If you set things up on your office computer, it won't appear on the projector computer. Use the share link inside the game to move your list over — or simply do your setup on the same device you'll plug into the projector.

New games are added regularly, and your feedback makes them better! Have an idea for a game your residents would love? Run into a problem or an obstacle? Email me at helloprojectorfirst@gmail.com — I read every message.
