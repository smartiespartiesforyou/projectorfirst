About Me

I created ProjectorFirst because I wanted to make tools that are easy for everyone to use — especially in senior care settings where clarity and simplicity matter most.

I used to be an Activity Director. My background is in psychology, with a degree in the subject, and I also hold a Master’s in Criminal Justice.

These experiences shaped my belief that technology should never get in the way of connection and fun. That’s why ProjectorFirst is designed to be big, simple, and stress-free.
