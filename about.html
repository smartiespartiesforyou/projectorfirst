About ProjectorFirst

ProjectorFirst was created to make technology more accessible for seniors and care communities. We focus on big fonts, simple layouts, and one-click apps that work beautifully on projectors and TVs.

Our goal is to support nursing homes, senior centers, and caregivers by providing easy-to-use digital activities that bring people together. No small buttons. No clutter. Just clear, fun tools.
